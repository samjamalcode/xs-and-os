Game Explanation

Tic Tac Toe is a classic pencil-and-paper game for two players. Played on a 3x3 grid, opponents take turns marking empty squares with Xs or Os. The objective is to get three of your symbols in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, before your opponent does. The game ends when one player achieves this goal, or if the entire grid is filled without a winner, resulting in a draw. It's a quick, strategic game often enjoyed by people of all ages.

Game Rules

In Tic Tac Toe, players alternate placing Xs and Os. The first to create a row of three of their marks wins. If the grid fills up without a winner, the game ends in a draw.
